Top 3 albums of 2022 thus far

in no particular order

"The Kick" by Foxes

It's so DANCEY and 80s. Her first album in 6 years is NEAUX disappointment. Bless yourselves with "Sister Ray," "Absolute," "Two Kinds of Silence," and her magnum opus "Sky Love." Thank you.

"In Real Life" by Mandy Moore

She has really found her voice. Baby let herself play around with a synth or two on this album after going away from pop music following her 2001 album. Still as wise as ever. Listen to "In Other Words" and "Four Moons" to FEEL again.

"Harry's House" by Harry Styles

His voice is wonderful. It simply is. His most pop album to date still has indie flair that I love. You must stream "Dayight," "Grapejuice," and "Keep Driving" if you need an example of good music.
